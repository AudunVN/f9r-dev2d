keybindings

  • throttle: arrow keys (left and right vector, up and down increase/decrease thrust)
  • throttle: shift, control, 'a', and 'd' (like KSP)
  • landing gear: 'g'
  • unclamp from launch clamp: space
  • show minimap: 'm' (toggle; to completely disable, check the 'minimap' option in the upper right corner.)
  • reset: 'r'
  • rcs toggle: 'q' (when rcs is on, you can tilt the craft even with engines off)
  • use center engine: '1'
  • use three engines: '3'
  • use all nine engines: '9' (not always possible)

tips

  • remember that you are not adjusting angle; you are adjusting the throttle vector, which takes a few moments to overcome the momentum of the rocket.
  • if you hit the ground too fast or at an angle, you will crash.
  • when you're ready to launch, don't forget to press space after you've throttled up to release the launch clamp
  • don't forget to extend the landing gear.
  • crashed? hit 'r'.
